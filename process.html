Your health is in good hands

We want you to be well informed and feel as comfortable as possible with us. That's why we've prepared a detailed guide to help you learn about the process of participating in our clinical trials.

Find a trial that works best for you

Take our brief personalized online survey to match you with an appropriate trial

Schedule an in person visit

After completing the survey use our calendar to select a day and time that works best for you to come visit our facility.

Your first visit (30mins-1hr)

You will meet with our highly trained personnel to qualify and walk you through what you can expect from participating in a clinical trial.

Your coordinator will ask questions about your health history, concomitant medications, ongoing treatment, and your lifestyle habits. We'll discuss the protocol in detail, such as appointment schedules, procedures, and medications if required. After you read and sign a copy of the informed consent, we will be able to schedule you for a trial.

Treatment begins (varies on the trial)

You will be thoroughly informed about the adverse effects and interactions with drugs and herbal supplements. You will be given all the necessary information about dosage and how to take your medications.

Final phase

Once you sign the informed consent and the trial begins, each of your visits will be documented. You will receive the investigational product from us if needed. We will also schedule all follow-up visits.
